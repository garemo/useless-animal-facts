Here are the the first set of animals:

BlueGill

Bluegills are fish that are not actually blue.

BillyGoat

Billy goats like to eat grass and are pretty friendly. They usually aren't named Billy though. While they may be friendly they can still bite.

Tiger

Tigers have stripes on them. They do not like to be petted. Most Tigers are not named Tony.

Lion

Most Lions names are not Leo. They also do not like to be petted.
